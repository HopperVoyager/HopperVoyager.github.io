Peer Tutoring is a method of instruction that involves students teaching other students.


What is the advantage to peer tutoring?

Tutors benefit by reteaching a subject that they have already mastered by reviewing the concepts and answering questions from their tutees. Students who are struggling learn from tutors because someone who is their age level can explain concepts in a different way from their adult educators.ch
